Security and Suggestions
How Security is maintained
At Vroomcar.in we make sure that the profile's of both the person offering and finding the ride is 100% complete. We have made sure that the services of vroomcar.in cannot be used until you complete your profile. The email id used for providing services at vroomcar.in is taken from the social login of the customer, which makes sure the email is verified for car owner and co-traveler. This is the first level of security. The personal information and the social networks of both the car owner and o-traveler is shared with each other so that before finalizing the trip you can check all the details to be completely assured. We also encourage the co-traveler to share the comments and ratings(coming soon) on vroomcar.in so that this can be used by the other co-traveler in future before he finalizes the trip.

Choose what you like
When you search for the ride at vroomcar.in we display all the rides available on the particular route. So you have full flexibility to choose the ride. We recommend you to check all the credentials and check the ratings available before you finalize the ride.

Complaints and Suggestions
With all the best practices and the security measures deployed and mentioned on the vroomcar.in, we would still be pleased to hear from you for any suggestions or complaints about the offering on the website or mobile app. Please feel from to contact us.
