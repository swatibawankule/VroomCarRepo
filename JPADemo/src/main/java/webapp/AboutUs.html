What is Vroom Car!!!
Traveling alone over long distances can be boring. You have an awesome car, with awesome music system, the irony you are traveling alone!!!
How about finding someone who is searching for a nice car to reach his place?
The good thing out of all this, you get a company and the important thing, you get that extra money which may not be very big, but who don't like saving few dollars.
@Vroom car we help you find a nice hearted person, who can accompany you to your place.
With our large pool of customers looking for a safe ride, we make sure that you get a perfect companion for your journey.

By the end you will not only get good money in your pocket, but you will get 2 new friends in your life - One the person who traveled with you, and other The vroomcar.in.

The Birth!
Rahul, the founder along with his wife was traveling over an awesome highway from Nagpur to Pune. He was traveling via his car as Trains, buses, Flights were tough to find in the season time in India.
Many others were doing the same thing.

His car had 2 extra seats left, which made him post about the empty seat on Facebook. It was tough to find, but finally thanks to Facebook friends list, the seats were occupied, the money was shared and rest is what you are looking at. Use Vroom Car because not everyone gets the co-traveller via friends list!!!
